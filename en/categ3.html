Orchid
It is an expensive plant, is found in all colors of the spectrum, and lives from seven days to two weeks. It means beautiful, i.e. the most beautiful, charming and attractive woman among women.
Clove
These curvy flowers symbolize infatuation and new love, and also signify beauty and pride. For some reason, the cloves didn't get what they deserved. But some people find them wonderful and amazing flowers.
Alstroemeria
Its large, strong, long-lasting and eye-catching petals symbolize friendship and fidelity. It is native to South America and has many buds on one stem
