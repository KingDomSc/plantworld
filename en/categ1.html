Potts
Also called devil's ivy, it needs moderate watering, tolerates dry weather, but prefers high humidity and should not be exposed to direct sunlight so as not to burn the tips of its leaves
Cactus
Aloe vera plants tolerate all conditions, placed by windows or near light sources
Sansevieria
One of the best plants for beginners in cultivation, it grows easily, dies with difficulty, adapts to all kinds of light and does not like a lot of water, watered at long intervals
